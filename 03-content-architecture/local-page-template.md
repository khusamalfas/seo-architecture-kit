# Local Page Template

## Purpose
Rank for `{service} + {city}` and capture local demand.

## Sections
1. City + service specific H1
2. Localised intro (city context)
3. Address, contact, working hours
4. Local reviews
5. Map / directions
6. Local pricing / offers
7. FAQ

## SEO
- GEO-modified title and H1.
- Local Business / Organization schema.
