# URL Rules

## Principles
- Human-readable, concise, keyword-aligned.
- One canonical URL per intent/cluster.
- Stable over time – avoid frequent renames.
- Lowercase, hyphen-separated, no special characters.

## General Rules
- No tracking parameters in canonical URLs.
- No file extensions (`.php`, `.html`).
- Avoid deep nesting where not needed.
- Keep URLs under ~115 characters when possible.

## Examples
- `/services/teeth-whitening/`
- `/blog/dental-implants/cost-process-recovery/`
- `/city/london/dentist/`

## GEO Logic
- One clear strategy: either subfolders (`/uk/`, `/de/`) or subdomains – but consistent.
- Local pages follow consistent structure: `/city/{city-name}/{service}/`.
